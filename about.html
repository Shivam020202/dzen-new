Our Journey

A story written in milestones

From a single treatment room to a trusted name in aesthetic dermatology, our path has been shaped by patience, science and an obsession with getting the details right.

  1. 2018

    The founding vision

    D'Zen Derma opens its doors with a promise: aesthetic care rooted in science, delivered with the calm of a sanctuary rather than the rush of a clinic.

  2. 2020

    Regenerative aesthetics

    We introduce advanced regenerative therapies — PRP, GFC and bio-stimulation — restoring radiance from within and setting a new standard for natural-looking results.

  3. 2022

    Training the industry

    Dr. Aarushi becomes a sought-after senior trainer, mentoring over 120 fellow doctors through masterclasses and fellowship programmes across the country.

  4. 2024

    Holistic wellness

    We expand into integrative wellness and functional medicine, treating skin as part of a whole-body approach to lasting vitality and confidence.

  5. Today

    A trusted sanctuary

    Thousands of transformations later, D'Zen Derma continues to grow — still guided by the same balance of science, artistry and genuine, personalised care.

Good to Know

Frequently asked questions

What treatments does D'Zen Derma offer?

We offer a full spectrum of aesthetic dermatology and wellness services — skin rejuvenation, HydraFacial, laser therapies, PRP and GFC, dermal fillers, anti-ageing protocols, regenerative aesthetics, body sculpting and integrative wellness programmes — each personalised to your skin and goals.

Are your treatments safe?

Yes. Every protocol is doctor-led, performed with medical-grade technology and evidence-based products. Safety, restraint and natural-looking results guide every decision, beginning with a thorough consultation and skin assessment.

Who are the doctors at D'Zen Derma?

The clinic is founded and medically directed by Dr. Aarushi Passi Bhandari, an aesthetic physician with an MD in Dermatology and a senior trainer to fellow doctors, alongside Rahul Bhandari, who leads brand and experience design.

Do you offer consultations before treatment?

Absolutely. Every journey begins with a personalised consultation where we assess your skin, understand your concerns and design a bespoke treatment plan. You can book online or by phone.

What makes D'Zen Derma different from other clinics?

We unite clinical excellence with a philosophy of balance — pairing science-led aesthetic dermatology with holistic wellness, regenerative technology and an unhurried, sanctuary-like experience.

Where is D'Zen Derma located?

D'Zen Derma is based in India and welcomes clients from your city and beyond. Use the Find Us link to view directions, or contact our team to plan your visit.
